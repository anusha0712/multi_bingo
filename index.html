📋 How to Play

🎯 Objective

Complete all 16 squares on your bingo card by correctly matching players/characters to categories.

🎮 How to Play

  • A random player/character appears at the top
  • Click on a category square that matches this player
  • If correct ✅ - the square turns green and you continue
  • If wrong ❌ - the square flashes red and you get a new player
  • Complete all 16 squares to win!

🃏 Special Actions

  • Skip: Avoid difficult players you don't know
  • Wildcard: Automatically complete any square (use once per game)

🏆 Strategy Tips

  • Save specific categories (like "Afghan Player") for obvious matches
  • Don't waste versatile players on common categories
  • Skip players you're unsure about
  • Use wildcard strategically for difficult categories

🔄 Sport Switching

Switching between sports will start a new game and reset your progress.
